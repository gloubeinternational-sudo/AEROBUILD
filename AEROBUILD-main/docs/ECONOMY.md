## BUSINESS RULES

- Every purchase creates a transaction.
- Every transaction is permanently stored.
- Every digital part belongs to one aircraft.
- Every aircraft has an independent economy.
- Users may own multiple digital parts.
- Teams may accumulate collective progress.
- Corporate accounts may purchase in bulk.
- No ownership rights are created unless explicitly defined by the legal model.
- Financial operations must be fully auditable.
