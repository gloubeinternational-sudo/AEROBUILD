## COMMUNITY

The platform is based on voluntary participation.

Users join the community to contribute, collaborate, learn, compete, and support aviation projects.

Participation does not automatically create ownership rights, investment rights, or financial claims.

Different participation models may be introduced under separate legal frameworks in the future.

---

## DIGITAL ASSETS

* Digital Parts
* User Profiles
* Achievements
* Reputation
* Certificates

Digital assets represent participation inside the platform ecosystem.

They do not constitute securities, equity, investment contracts, ownership rights, or financial instruments unless explicitly established under future legal documentation.
